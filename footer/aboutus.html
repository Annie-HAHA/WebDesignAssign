ABOUT US

Mr Bakery is a family owned bakery shop from Malaysia. We have been serving our authentic homemade bakery since 1985 and it has passed down from generation to generation. Our bakery is located at the busy street of Petaling Jaya, Kuala Lumpur.

Our Story

Mr Bakery was founded by Mr. Bake in 1985. He came up with this business idea when his wife was fighting cancer. He quitted his job after that. To encourage her from the illness, he decided to make some cakes with healthy ingredients that filled them with love. Yet, his wife successfully defeated cancer after all. Due to this incident, he believes a healthy lifestyle comes with nutritious food.


Business Philosophy

We sincerely believe that “Health Is Wealth” is our priority. We care what we serve for the communities. Choosing organic ingredients which don’t contain any preservatives that will cause harm to the body in the long run is the promise we give.

What we do

We have a selection of bakery choices. To survive and adapt to the changes of the era, we always innovate our recipe without changing the original taste. Not only cakes we sell, we also provide different services like bakery class for those who are interested.
We also provide an online platform for ordering cakes and delivery service.

    Our vision & mission

  • We aim to expand our business all over Malaysia with 10 branches by the year 2040
  • We serve people with the best homemade bakery with our whole heart, mind and soul
  • Improvise in quality, safety and value
